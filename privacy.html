Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

The short version

Floating Head does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Your camera feed stays entirely on your device.

Camera access

Floating Head requires access to your Mac's camera to display a live video feed in a floating window. The camera feed is rendered locally using macOS AVFoundation and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere. When you use Continuity Camera with your iPhone, the same applies — the feed stays on your device.

Data collection

Floating Head collects no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports
  • No cookies or tracking
  • No network requests

Local storage

Your preferences (window size, shape, border color, mirror mode, etc.) are saved locally on your Mac using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Third-party services

Floating Head uses no third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks. It is built entirely with Apple's native frameworks (SwiftUI, AppKit, AVFoundation).

Website

The Floating Head website at floatinghead.app does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking scripts. The "Try it" camera demo on the homepage runs entirely in your browser — no video data is transmitted.

Children's privacy

Since Floating Head collects no data, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "last updated" date.
