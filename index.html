What
is
this
thing?

It's a floating circle with your face in it.

Open the app. Pick your camera. A transparent, always-on-top circle appears with your webcam feed. Drag it anywhere. Start your screen recording, stream, or presentation. That's it.

No complex OBS setup. No video editing. No layers, scenes, or fifteen-step tutorials. Just you, floating.

The good stuff

◯

Three shapes

Circle, rounded square, or hexagon. Because why not hexagon.

↔

Any size

Tiny and subtle or large and impossible to ignore. 100px to 600px.

⚪

Custom borders

White, black, or any color you want. Adjustable width. Get fancy.

Mirror mode

Flip the feed so you look the way you expect to look.

Stays visible across every macOS desktop and Space. Always there.
